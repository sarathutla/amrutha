Quality Is a Responsibility

At Amrutha, food safety and quality are not treated as final checks. They are built into every stage of our sourcing, processing and packaging operations.

Our Quality Approach

We follow a preventive, process-driven approach to quality control, ensuring consistency, hygiene and safety across batches.

Process-Based Quality

Quality at Amrutha is ensured through defined operating procedures rather than reliance on end-stage inspection alone. Each step of processing is designed to minimise risk and variation.

Consistency Focus

Our systems aim to deliver uniform grain quality, cooking performance and safety parameters across every production batch.

Hygiene & Food Safety Practices

Clean processing environments and disciplined handling practices are essential to food safety.

Clean Processing Environment

Processing and packaging areas are maintained under controlled hygiene conditions to reduce contamination risks and maintain product integrity.

Handling Discipline

Defined handling practices are followed to ensure that rice is not exposed to avoidable contamination during movement and storage.

Quality Checks & Controls

Multiple checks are carried out to ensure compliance with internal standards.

Raw Material Checks

Paddy is inspected for moisture levels, cleanliness and grain condition before being accepted for processing.

Batch-Level Inspection

Processed rice batches are evaluated for grain uniformity, appearance and overall quality before packaging.

Packaging Integrity

Final packaging is checked to ensure proper sealing, labelling accuracy and protection during storage and transport.

Storage & Dispatch Controls

Finished goods are stored under appropriate conditions to maintain freshness until dispatch.

Compliance & Regulatory Alignment

Amrutha aligns its operations with applicable Indian food safety regulations.

FSSAI Compliance

Operations are carried out in accordance with guidelines prescribed under the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).

Continuous Improvement

Quality systems are periodically reviewed and refined to strengthen safety, hygiene and process consistency.

Quality You Can Depend On

Amrutha’s commitment to quality is driven by responsibility — to consumers, trade partners and the food ecosystem we operate in.
