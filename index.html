Why We Choose Single Polished Rice

Single polishing is a conscious balance — retaining more of the grain’s natural layers without compromising everyday cooking and taste.

Unlike fully polished rice, single polished rice is processed gently, leaving more of the grain intact rather than stripping it down completely.

This approach keeps the rice closer to its natural form while still ensuring it cooks well and fits comfortably into regular meals.
