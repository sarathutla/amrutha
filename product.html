What is Single Polished Rice?

Single polished rice undergoes minimal surface polishing, unlike fully polished rice which is repeatedly processed for appearance.

Minimal Processing

After milling, the rice is polished only once to remove excess bran while retaining more of the grain’s natural nutrients and structure.

Balanced Approach

Single polishing offers a balance between nutrition and cooking quality, making it suitable for regular Indian meals.

Single Polished vs Fully Polished Rice

Understanding the difference helps make informed choices.

Single Polished Rice

• Minimal polishing
• Higher nutrient retention
• Natural grain texture
• Balanced taste and softness
• Suitable for daily consumption

Fully Polished Rice

• Multiple polishing cycles
• Reduced nutritional value
• Focus on visual whiteness
• Less natural grain character
• Primarily appearance-driven
